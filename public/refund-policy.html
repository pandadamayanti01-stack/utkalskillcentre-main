Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2026

Thank you for subscribing to Utkal Skill Centre. We are committed to providing premium digital study notes, textbooks, and practice tests. Please read our policy regarding refunds and cancellations carefully.

1. Digital Products Policy

Because our services are digital and provide immediate, complete access to textbooks, handwritten notes, mock tests, and AI Tutor features immediately upon successful payment, subscriptions are generally non-refundable once purchased.

2. Duplicate Payments & Failed Transactions

If you experience any transaction issues during checkout:

  • Failed Transactions: If your bank account is debited but the subscription remains inactive, please contact us. If verified, the payment gateway (Razorpay) will automatically refund the amount to your source account within 5 to 7 business days.
  • Duplicate Purchases: In the event that you are accidentally charged twice for the same subscription package due to a connection drop or browser refresh, please submit a request to our support email within 48 hours. Accidental duplicate payments will be refunded in full.

3. Cancellation Policy

  • Subscription Cancellations: You can cancel your subscription plan at any time through your profile settings or by contacting our support team.
  • Access after Cancellation: If you cancel your subscription before the end of the current billing cycle (e.g., monthly billing), your premium access will remain active until the end of that billing period. Your account will not be billed for subsequent cycles.

4. Sibling Account Accidental Upgrades

We understand that children might use a shared device. Parents who verify that an upgrade was purchased accidentally by a child without parental consent may request a refund within 24 hours of checkout, provided that no test series papers have been fully attempted. Such cases are evaluated individually by our customer support desk.
