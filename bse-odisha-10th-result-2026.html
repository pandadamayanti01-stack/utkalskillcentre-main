How to check Odisha 10th Result?

  1. Visit any official website: bseodisha.ac.in or bseodisha.nic.in
  2. Click on the link for "Annual HSC Examination Result 2026".
  3. Enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth.
  4. Click on Submit to view your marksheet.
  5. Download and take a printout for future reference.
