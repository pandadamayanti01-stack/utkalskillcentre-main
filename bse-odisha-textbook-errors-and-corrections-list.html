⚠️ Famous Textbook Blunders Flagged

Some of the most publicized errors identified in the official print editions of the school books include:

🏛️ Karnataka Assembly Photo

In the Class 8 history/civics chapter, the picture of the Karnataka Legislative Assembly was mistakenly printed with the label of the Odisha Legislative Assembly.

⛰️ Niyamgiri Hills Location

The geographical position of the sacred Niyamgiri Hills (originally in Rayagada and Kalahandi, Odisha) was wrongfully shown inside Jharkhand state.

📍 Ganjam District Mislabel

Ganjam district in southern Odisha was mistakenly named as "Berhampur district" in official mapping pages (Berhampur is a city within Ganjam, not a district).

➕ Math Sign Mismatches

Several Class 7 and Class 8 algebra worksheets contained missing mathematical operators and sign mismatches, making them unsolvable as printed.
