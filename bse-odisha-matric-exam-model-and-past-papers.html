Frequently Asked Questions

How can I download BSE Odisha Class 10 previous year question papers?

Official Class 10 past question papers can be downloaded from bseodisha.ac.in. Additionally, chapter-wise questions and solved practice tests are available on the Utkal Skill Centre app.

What is the exam structure for the Class 10 Board exam in 2026?

The exam consists of 100 Marks for major subjects (50 MCQs and 5 Subjective questions of 10 Marks each). Science and Social Science are 50 Marks each.
