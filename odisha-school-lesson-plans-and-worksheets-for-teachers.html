How to Generate Materials Online

  1. Open the Utkal Skill Centre app in your web browser.
  2. Log in with your educator credentials (or use testing pass 9876543210).
  3. Navigate to the **Gundulu AI Workspace** inside the Educator Studio.
  4. Choose your target Grade (Class 1 to 10) and the chapter from the textbooks catalog.
  5. Click **Generate** to get formatted materials and print directly from your browser.
