About July 2026 MTS

The **MTS (Monthly Test Series)** is a free, syllabus-aligned online evaluation platform designed for Odisha Board students to test their learning progress regularly. Master your curriculum with detailed scores, step-by-step solutions, and state-wide rank leaderboards.

The July 2026 Monthly Test Series will run from **July 6th to July 11th, 2026** (excluding Sunday, July 5th, which is a lockout rest day). Check the class-wise timetables and exam durations below.

MTS Timetable & Exam Durations
