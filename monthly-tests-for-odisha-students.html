Monthly Tests

Monthly tests for Odisha students

Monthly tests are a practical way to keep students engaged because they create a clear short-term goal. Students can prepare, attempt, and review progress without waiting for annual exams.

Open Utkal Skill Centre

Why monthly tests help

Regular confidence checks

Students see whether they are improving every month instead of guessing.

Parent visibility

Parents understand progress better when there is a clear test result and routine.

Better study discipline

Tests encourage revision and chapter completion in a more natural way.

How this fits Utkal Skill Centre

  • Students can practice and then attempt monthly tests
  • Tests support habit-building and repeat app visits
  • Short-cycle progress is easier to communicate than long-term promises
